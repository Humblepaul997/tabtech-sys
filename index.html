Farm input Distribution Systems is a web -based systems which manages all farm inputs under subsidy program, Famers are registered from sectors,within their respective communities.

The Systems includes Traders and Agro dealers Registration and monitoring of all transactions under Subsidy Program.
The Systems has a number of funcionalities to enable effeciency and accountability of Subsidy Farm input distribution program.
The Sytem monitors all Transaction in different levells and provides reports to both end users (Public), stakeholders (Administrators), and all other interested parties.
Funcions includes but not Limited to:
1.registration of Traders 
2. Registration of Famers 
3.Farm input Distribution management
4. Tracking of Transportation of farm input products
5. Tracking and Management of farm input beneficiaries
6. Distribution accounatbility and Inventoy System.
   All transactions are monitored and tracked by the system. 
This system has a user interface which is accessible by all users and other stakeholders.
